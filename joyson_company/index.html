Client overview 

There are several entities related with our client, the very first is Winecommune LLC that is a popular online auction company started in 1999 and the main company where some others borned. In 2004 the founders recognized the shift towards online wine sales and expanded the offerings to wine enthusiasts through WineZap an online search engine that allowed them to compare prices and retailers. Finally, in 2005, after the brand and the membership for Winecommune and WineZap were built up, a new entity appears on the scene JJ Buckley Fine Wines as the wine retail arm of WineCommune LLC. This last entity complete’s the founders’ original vision to provide wine enthusiasts with multiple channels to buy or sell fine wine within a single online marketplace.

JJB Topics 

Since the beginning of our business relation with JJ Buckley, Cecropia has been working in some sites related with the different business pillars of Winecommune LLC. As we mentioned on the client’s overview they based and supported their business with online tools, sites or systems.
JJbuckley site is in a transition process where is going to change its programming language from ColdFusion to ASP .NET C#.
  • JJBuckley - Cecropia
    JJ Buckley.com the retail arm
     
  • Customer's profiles

    We provide solution to the JJ Buckley’s final customers to handle basic information like general information, addresses, credit cards and a management system to handle all wines previously bought and stored, like his personal inventory.

     
  • Multiwarehouse inventory

    Since JJ Buckley required to have more than one location to store and distribute their products, the system changed to handle existences on the different warehouses. All affectations since the entrances or outs of inventory are handled and managed by warehouse.

     
  • Checkout process

    i. Communication with third parties:
    1. Shipping costs API: Communication with the different providers can be established in order to fulfill the needs of the final customer in terms of shipping. GSO FEDEX UPS
    2. Credit Card Validation: Credit Card validation using third-party APIs from processors like Authorize.net and Paypal.

    ii. Internal Order formalization:
    In order to keep an accurate inventory and orders log the process is directly sent to Winecommune.com.

JJB Topics 

The business management system is used for the most of the common processes on WineCommune LLC. This system is of course secure and is limited by roles and users permissions, and you can get information by reports that can be downloadables, screen displayed or sended by email. All these applying all business rules that most of the time should be customized.
  • Winecommune - Cecropia
    Warehouse Management
     
  • Inventory Receiving

    The system controls all inventory inclussions and check this against the existing purchase orders. Also every unit of inventory is being identified by the system using a license plate to identify it. A unit of inventory can be a box full bottles or a single bottle.

     
  • Inventory Control

    The whole warehouse where the bottles are storaged exists logically, in this way every inventory unit can be associated to an specific location facilitating the pick labor when a bottle is requested on an order. Besides its location every inventory unit has associated the quantity that represents in terms of the whole inventory, original amount contained on the creation and the remaining quantity.

     
  • Inventory Consolidation

    The inventory periodically is being consolidated and counted bottle by bottle to ensure the accuracy on the inventory that is being offered on JJ Buckley and also the cost variation on broken or missing bottles.


  • Winecommune - Cecropia
    Warehouse Management
     
  • Sending pack units

    The packing process of the orders is also being controlled by the system, guiding the users to the right locations to find the bottles and complete the packing process on the pack table where a report per ship unit is create as the pack list and prepare the final details to ship the packslips.

     
  • Operations

    The majority of the personal on JJ Buckley uses a mobile device to fullfil the most of the actions we described inside the warehouse, this mobile module is a great module to facilitate in a secure way the operation and also empowered the users.

     
  • Control Console

    A control console gives the managers the warehouse visibility of the processes that are being performed by the personal on the of the warehouse.
